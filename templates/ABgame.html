Hello! Let's play a game!
A guess number game.

You need to guess 4 different numbers.
If there is a number in the answer, I'll print out 'B'.
If there is a number in the answer and at the correct index, I'll print out 'A'.

Example:
answer: 2583 (Will not show in the game.)

guess1: 
    0123
    1A1B
guess2: 
    2537
    2A1B
